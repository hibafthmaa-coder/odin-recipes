Sadhya

Sadhya is a traditional Kerala feast served on a banana leaf with a variety of vegetarian dishes.

It is usually prepared during festivals and special occasions like Onam.

Ingredians

  • Rice
  • Coconut
  • Yogurt
  • Vegetables
  • Curry leaves
  • Banana chips

Steps

  1. Wash and cook the rice until soft.
  2. Prepare vegetable dishes such as avial, thoran, and sambar.
  3. Make side dishes like pickle, pappadam, and banana chips.
  4. Arrange all dishes on a banana leaf.
  5. Serve with rice and enjoy the traditional feast.
