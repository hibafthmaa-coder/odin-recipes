Biriyani

Biryani is a flavorful rice dish made with aromatic spices, basmati rice, and meat or vegetables.

It is one of the most popular dishes in India and many other countries.

Ingredians

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken or mutton
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Biryani spices

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt and spices.
  2. Cook the basmati rice until partially done.
  3. Fry onions until golden brown.
  4. Cook the marinated chicken with onions and tomatoes.
  5. Layer the rice and chicken mixture in a pot.
  6. Cook on low heat until the flavors blend together.
  7. Serve hot with raita or salad.
